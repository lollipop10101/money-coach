#### 1. E-Mode (Efficiency Mode)
- Designed for correlated asset pairs (collateral + borrowed asset move together)

- One-click leveraged positions without manual looping
- Borrow low-APY asset, use as collateral to farm amplified rewards
- Works with LSTs (Liquid Staked Assets) and LP tokens

#### 3. Isolation Mode
- New assets listed as isolated with a designated debt ceiling
- Isolated assets can **only** borrow stablecoins approved by NAVI governance
- Protects the main pool from bad debt from high-volatility assets

- Available on all pools
- Must be initiated and settled within the same block
- Two modes: `flashloan` (funds + receipt to initiator) and `flashloan_with_AccountCap`
- Use cases: arbitrage, collateral swap, debt refinancing

## 2. Advanced Carry Trade Strategies

### 2.1 Core Carry Trade Mechanics

The basic carry trade on NAVI:
- **Supply APR** earned on deposited assets
- **Borrow APR** paid on loaned assets
- **Net carry** = Supply APR − Borrow APR

Typical profitable pairs (stablecoin-heavy):
- Supply USDC/U STD → borrow USDT → deploy USDT to higher-yield farm
- The spread between supply and borrow rates determines carry margin

### 2.2 Loop Staking Strategy (vSUI/SUI E-Mode)

This is the most popular sophisticated strategy on NAVI, leveraging the vSUI/SUI E-Mode pair:

```
Step 1: Stake SUI → receive vSUI (via Volo)
Step 2: Supply vSUI to NAVI as collateral (LTV 75% normally, 90% in E-Mode)
Step 3: Borrow SUI (up to 90% LTV in E-Mode)
Step 4: Re-stake borrowed SUI → receive more vSUI
Step 5: Supply vSUI to NAVI again (compounding)
Step 6: Use staking rewards + yield farming to repay borrowed SUI over time
```

**Why it works:**
- vSUI staking yield: ~8-15% APY (varies with network conditions)
- E-Mode allows 90% LTV (vs 75% standard) — almost no capital drag
- Net position: exposure to SUI price appreciation + staking yield, while borrowing cost is minimized
- The vSUI/SUI price correlation keeps liquidation risk low in normal conditions

**OKX Wallet documentation confirms:** "Enter the number of times you would like to leverage... your SUI will be staked for vSUI, which will be lent as collateral to borrow SUI, which will be staked for vSUI, etc."

### 2.3 Leveraged Looping with Automatic Leverage Vault

NAVI's Automatic Leverage Vault automates the looping process:

1. Deposit collateral asset (e.g., USDC or SUI)
2. Vault borrows a low-APR asset (e.g., USDC borrow at ~5% if utilization low)
3. Uses borrowed to buy more collateral → repeat up to target leverage
4. Earns amplified farming rewards on the total position

**Risk:** Multiplying positions multiplies both yield AND liquidation risk. A 3x leveraged position means a 33% price move against you triggers liquidation.

### 2.4 Cross-Collateral Strategy

Strategy: Supply asset A as collateral → borrow stablecoin → deploy stablecoin to high-yield farm

**Example:**
- Supply 10,000 USDC worth of WETH
- Borrow 7,500 USDC (75% LTV)
- Deploy 7,500 USDC to FlowX SUI/USDC pool (~100% APR per recent data)
- Net yield = farming APR − borrow APR
- If farming yields 100% and borrow costs 5%, net carry = 95%

**With leverage multiplier:**
- Using 2x leverage on the same trade: 20,000 USDC position, 15,000 borrowed
- Yield scales but so does risk

## 4. Risk Management

### 4.1 Liquidation Mechanics

From NAVI GitBook documentation:

- **Health Factor (HF)** = Σ(Supply_i in USD × Liquidation Threshold_i) / Total Borrows in USD
- **HF < 1** → Liquidation risk
- When liquidated: **up to 35%** of borrower's debt repaid in one event
- Liquidator receives collateral value + **liquidation fee** (asset-specific)

**Liquidation thresholds for key assets:**

| Asset | Liq. Threshold | Liq. Bonus |
|---|---|---|
| SUI | 80% | 10% |
| USDC (Wormhole) | 85% | 8% |
| vSUI | ~80% | ~8% |
| WETH | ~80% | ~8% |
| NAVX | Low (~50%?) | Higher |

> **Practical rule:** Keep Health Factor above 1.5 at minimum. Above 2.0 for safety during high volatility.

### 4.2 LTV Management Guidelines

**Safe operating zones:**

| Risk Level | Max LTV Used | Health Factor Target |
|---|---|---|
| Conservative | 50% of max LTV | >2.5 |
| Moderate | 70% of max LTV | >1.8 |
| Aggressive | 85%+ of max LTV | >1.2 |
| Dangerous | 95%+ of max LTV | ~1.0 |

**Example:** SUI max LTV = 75%. Conservative use = 37.5% LTV, Moderate = 52.5%, Aggressive = 63.75%.

### 4.3 Volatility Risk

- During market crashes, collateral assets (SUI, ETH, BTC) can drop 30-50% rapidly
- A leveraged position that seems safe at 2x HF can go underwater fast
- **Sui's fast finality** (sub-second) helps — liquidations can be executed quickly, reducing cascade risk vs slower chains
- **Strategy:** Reduce leverage before major market events (macro announcements, FOMC, etc.)

### 4.4 vSUI Depeg Risk

- If vSUI trades at discount to SUI, it signals potential trouble
- Monitor vSUI/SUI exchange rate — deviation >2% from 1:1 warrants attention
- Diversify staking across Volo, Haedal (haSUI) to reduce single-protocol risk

### 4.5 Monitoring Tools

NAVI provides real-time health monitoring via their app dashboard. Key metrics visible:
- Liquidation value of positions
- Distance from liquidation threshold
- Acceptable range for token value fluctuation
- Projected liquidation price if assets rise/fall to threshold

**NAVI open API:** `https://open-api.naviprotocol.io/contractconfigs` — for real-time parameter access

### 4.6 Insurance

- **Nexus Mutual** coverage available for smart contract risk on major protocols
- Coverage typically covers: hack exploits, oracle failures, smart contract bugs
- Not available for: market risk, depegging, liquidation events
- Consider coverage if deploying >$50K in NAVI positions
